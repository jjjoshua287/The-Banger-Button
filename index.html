The Banger Button

Lead Developer: Josh Falzone

Introduction

Long Before time, a fabled legendary button appeared...

People assumed it naturally had powers for some unknown reasons.

The Button started giving off cosmic energy...

...that's when the people decided they needed to investigate it.

Conflict Arises

The button's cosmic rays were overpowering the villagers trying to defeat it.

They brought in heavy artillery in order to create it, however...

when they fired the 1st shot...

A small click was heard.

It sent out a shockwave and sound so devastating, the village ceased to exist in that instant

The Aftermath

The Button had completly disintegrated the village.

The button then left.

Present Day

The button was discovered at the Institute of Extreme Bangers

The scientists there worked with historians to understand The Button

The Legend of The Button

The Historians pointed out an old legend correlating to a button

Legend Has it that 1 button was deemed so "banger" that it destroyed an entire civilization

Your Part

The scientists theorized if you pushed the button a certain amount of times...


It'd stop having the ability of being able to destroy civilization
